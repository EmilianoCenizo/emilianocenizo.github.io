A bit about me

I'm 34 years old, born in La Pampa, Argentina; but living in Amsterdam, NL for the last 2 years with my girlfriend Gisela and our cats Sylvanas and Lucy.

Some quick facts about me: I have been working in the "regular software" industry for the last 10 years as a backend developer mostly, and since a few years back as a team lead / engineering manager for some big companies. I really feel comfortable both dealing with code AND also around people.

I LOVE videogames, and everything related to them - the gameplay analysis, the music (I started playing violin just to try to get some favourite osts), their cultural implications, everything. 80% of my clothing is videogame related, and of course, videogames are the reason I choose this career path.

For the last 4-5 years, after my regular 9-6 full time job, I have been doing a lot of prototypes on Unity; I also managed to attend a few gamejams, and after I graduated (december 2019) I started consuming a lot of more theory over game design - both on books and online content. I even managed to get into GDC this year (as an attendee of course).
